our story

DISCOVER THE UNKNOWN. Without fear. With reasonable prices.

Evertything starts with an idea.


TravelX has been invented by us at 2019 during the Spaceapps Challanges 2019 Ankara. We worked really hard and won the "most creative project" award. After that, Nasa wanted to improve our project and called us to America and we went there.

After establishing our project, we went to our separate ways only to be united again when the first trans-neptunian space station was launched at 2030. At that day, we were not talented high schoolers anymore, we were professionals, we kept our passion and we knew exactly what we wanted to do.

We went through very hard times but the obstacles made us only stronger. Now the company operates over more than %60 of kuiper belt, reaching to the farthest edges; operating 89 space shutlles and providing you personalized, well-planed trips you could only dream of.

We put our ideals into everything we do, for a better and ever-developing world. The company has been an avid sponsor of various scientific organisations and more than 3213 big startups since our foundation. We care about our customers, our products are carefully planned both for you and the environment.
